Here is TrainsToronto's Guide to creating your own ads.


INSTRUCTIONS:

1. Create an ad with a 375x500 resolution in .png format.

2. Edit the [WALLAD].csv file in Notepad, and rename NAMEOFTEXTURE.png
to the name of your file. Leave .png extension in the file.

3. Click save as, and save your new .csv file. Be careful when replacing files.
